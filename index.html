INDUSTRY 4.0 SOLUTIONS
Industrial IoT provides an opportunity to customize, control and automate the collection of critical data without interfering with operations. Using smart sensors, the facility managers are able to automate the collection of vital data, including from the invisible and hard-to-reach areas. This enables the organization to gain insights into a wide range of equipment and environmental conditions, hence an opportunity to reduce ineficiencies, failures, production costs and energy consumption. This assets digitalization process leads to the creation of the so-called digital twins.
