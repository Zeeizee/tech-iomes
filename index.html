INDUSTRY 4.0 SOLUTIONS
Industrial IoT provides an opportunity to customize, control and automate the collection of critical data without interfering with operations. Using smart sensors, the facility managers are able to automate the collection of key process and plant data, including from the invisible and hard-to-reach areas. Sophisticated data analytics enable the organization to gain insights into a wide range of equipment and environmental conditions, hence an opportunity to reduce inefficiencies, failures, production costs and energy consumption. This assets digitalization process leads to the creation of the so-called digital twins.

Our digital product line called TECH-IOMES offers solutions that transpose our domain expertise into machine learning models: building up knowledge of your industrial process and systems through their data, in real time TECH-IOMES provides analytical results about quality of finished products, failure prediction and malfunctioning alerts. Then, this digital twin will be your new platform for optimizations.
